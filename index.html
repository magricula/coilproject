About This Project

This Collaborative Online International Learning (COIL) project connects students from Canada and Romania to explore how identity is shaped through social practices and media narratives.

Project Goals

  • Compare everyday social practices
  • Analyze media representation
  • Reflect on cultural identity
  • Encourage intercultural dialogue
