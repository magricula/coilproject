    • About This Project

    This Collaborative Online International Learning (COIL) project brings together students from Canada and Romania to explore how identity is shaped through everyday experiences, social practices, and media narratives.

    By sharing perspectives and comparing cultural realities, this project highlights both differences and common ground between communities across borders.

    Project Goals

    • 🌍 Compare everyday social practices
    • 🎬 Analyze media representation
    • 🧠 Reflect on cultural identity
    • 🤝 Encourage intercultural dialogue
